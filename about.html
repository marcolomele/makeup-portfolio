About Me

I'm a professional makeup artist based in Milan, Italy, specializing in fashion, editorial, and bridal makeup. With over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry, I've worked with top fashion brands, photographers, and brides to create stunning looks that enhance natural beauty.

My approach combines technical expertise with artistic creativity, ensuring each client receives a personalized experience that makes them feel confident and beautiful.

Experience

I've had the privilege of working with prestigious fashion houses, editorial magazines, and celebrity clients. My work has been featured in Vogue Italia, Elle, and various international publications.

Specialties

  • Fashion & Editorial Makeup
  • Bridal & Special Occasion
  • Commercial & Photography
  • Skincare & Beauty Consulting
  • Makeup Workshops & Training

Philosophy

I believe that makeup should enhance your natural beauty, not mask it. Every face is unique, and I take pride in creating looks that celebrate individuality while achieving the desired aesthetic.
